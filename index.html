What services do you offer and what tech do you use?

We design and build websites, mobile apps and APIs. We handle UI/UX, cloud hosting and DevOps too. Our main stack is React (web), React Native (mobile), Java with Spring Boot and Python with FastAPI. We ship with Docker and Kubernetes on AWS, Azure or GCP.

How do you plan timelines and pricing?

We start with a short call to define the scope. Then we share a simple plan with milestones and a clear estimate. Fixed price for a clear scope; time and materials when things may change. Most MVPs take 4–8 weeks.

Will you sign an NDA and keep our data safe?

Yes. We sign NDAs and you own the code. We follow OWASP, do code reviews and automated security checks. We can align with GDPR or ISO rules. Access and secrets use least‑privilege.

How do you deliver work, and do you support after launch?

We work in weekly sprints and show demos. Every release is tested and documented. After launch we offer support plans for monitoring, bug fixes and small updates.
